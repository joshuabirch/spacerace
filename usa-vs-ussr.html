USA vs Soviet Union

Learn more about the political conflict between the USA and the Soviet Union during the Space Race...

Let's Start By Watching this Video...

It teaches you the basics of the timeline that was the Space Race including the political fued between the United States and the Soviet Union

The United States were the biggest asset to the Space Race and the Cold War due to them assisting any country who would be opposed by communism, aka the Soviet Union. Furthermore, the United States were constantly developing their military technology to fight the Soviet's while also developing technology to go to the Moon. Nazi German scientists assisted to the project as a whole including lead designer wernher von Braun who developed the V2 rocket in the second World War. The Americans eventually made it to space after Yuri Gagarin pipped them to the post in 1961. Despite multiple crippling investment, NASA finally pleased the world by being the first to reach the Moon.

The Soviets were continuing their dominance on Eastern Europe, securing land and further populating their political agenda. Doing this was easy, protecting eastern sovreignty with military aid while also assisting worldwide conflicts where the United States happened to be too. Their journey to space starting in 1961 with their Vostok rocket lead by Sergei Korolev, putting Yuri Gagarin in a two week orbit around the Earth before returning. However, multiple setbacks in deaths and departures of staff meant that the Soviet's push to the Moon proved to be too costly with time being the main factor. The Americans reached the Moon before the Soviet's and from there the work petered out despite a few attempts which lead to multiple deaths. The Space Race and the Cold War slowly wound down and the Soviet's were 'defeated'.
